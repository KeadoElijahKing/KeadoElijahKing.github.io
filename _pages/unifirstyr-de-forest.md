Hello and welcome to my De-Forest Station page. This project was a group project. The game is a tower defence game where the player places towers and traps to defeat the enemies, stopping them from entering the city and killing everyone.

{% include gallery id="gallery_Xylems" layout="third" thumb_height="180px" %}
These are the main enemies. They are called Xylems, they are people who have been taken over by a virus turning them into trees. The first image is of an early stage infection, and the next is that of a late stage Xylem. 

{% include gallery id="gallery_Towers" layout="third" thumb_height="180px" %}
Here I am showing the towers I created for the game. The first one is an Archer Tower, he is in a nook shooting arrows down on the Xylems. The sceond is a Heavy Machine Gunner, able to shoot from a distance and deal good damage. The third is a pistol tower, he doesn't have the range of the Machine Gunner but deals great damage. The next one is a Baseball Tower, he needs the Xylems to be close to deal damage. Then we have the Samurai, he deals high damage at low range. 

{% include gallery id="gallery_Thingsthathit" layout="third" thumb_height="180px" %}
Here we have the projectiles and weapons. The first is of an arrow that the archer uses. Then we have a bullet projectile for the Gunner and Pistol Towers. Then we have the sword that the Samurai uses.

{% include gallery id="gallery_Barriers" layout="third" thumb_height="180px" %}
These are the Barriers that blockade the city stopping the Xylems. If they take enough damage, teh break and then we move onto the next level. Here there are three on display, they progressively get more damaged showing how long the War has gone on.  

{% include gallery id="gallery_Traps" layout="third" thumb_height="180px" %}
Here I have my traps. These traps are placed along the path the Xylems follow, they deal high damage but fade after time. We have an Oil pit lit on fire, a spike pit where the Xylems fall into, then a spike bomb that sends spikes in all directions. 

{% include gallery id="gallery_Roads" layout="third" thumb_height="180px" %}
Then we have the roads, like the barriers they progressively get worse as the game continues. They are the path the Xylems follow. 

{% include gallery id="gallery_Others" layout="third" thumb_height="180px" %}
Finally we have our screens. The first one is the win screen. When you succesfully beat back the Xylems, this screen will appear. Then we have the loss screen and then the title screen. They all have the same background, of the ground, sky, clouds and trees. they all have a Xylem and Huamn in them too, in different positions based on the screen. Then surronding the text in the title and win screen, there are vines with flowers showing victory through life. The loss screen has thorned bushes around showing loss. Then we have an image of the game in progress, I am responsible for the lighting. The little lights are fireflies and the move around the level.
