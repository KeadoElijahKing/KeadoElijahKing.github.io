My name is Paul-Ryan Kilby and I am a game dev studnet currently attending the Ulster University Magee campus studying BSc Game Design and Development starting in September 2025. 

My main focus as a studnet is primarily a 3D artist, but I am aiming to be a generalist working in many sectors of the game design industry. My main strength that I have improved in since beginning my studies is in 3D modelling and animation. I have improved in every sector and will continue to do so. 

I am well versed in many softwares, I can competently use Blender, Substance Painter, Unity, Visual Studio C#, Photoshop, Libresprite and GitHub. I have been involved in and developed many projects as can be seen throughout this portfolio. 

I hope to one day start my own company, but I wish to join a professional gaming company to gain expereince and an understanding on how a gaming company opeartes. The main role I would like to have is that of a 3D artist and animator.

I have loved games since a child, I remember playing Rayman Revolution as a child with my Grandfater on his PS2 and being captivated by the story and mechanics. Then I was gifted a PS2 for christmas alongside what is now my favourite game, Kingdom Hearts. I have played many games, from Mass Effect and Dragon age, to Call Of Duty and Battlefront on Every generation of console. Playing these games has driven me to make games just as good for the player. I want to create games with no microtransactions and if there is to be DLC's, I want them to be free or very cheap. 

I am excited to learn as much as possible and bring my ideas to life. I am currently seeking any oppurtunity to enter into the industry where my skillset can compliment that of my peers and I can grow as a developer and take on new challenges.
